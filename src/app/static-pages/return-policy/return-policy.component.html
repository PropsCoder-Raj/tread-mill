Return Policy

Thank you for shopping with Dogshouse. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, please review our Return Policy outlined below.

1. Return Period

You may return most items within 30 days of receipt for a full refund. Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items, such as personalized products and clearance items, cannot be returned. Please check the product description for return eligibility.

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, contact our customer service team at support@dogshouse.com to obtain a Return Authorization Number (RAN). Include the RAN with your return shipment.

4. Refunds

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method once we receive and inspect the returned item. Shipping charges are non-refundable.

5. Exchanges

We do not offer exchanges at this time. If you need a different item, please return the original item and place a new order.

6. Shipping Costs

You are responsible for the cost of return shipping, unless the return is due to an error on our part or a defective product.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Return Policy, please contact us at support@dogshouse.com.
